Services In Details

International SEM

Why is it crucial for any company ready to export their product, to count with a comprehensive strategy addressing new markets in a local way? First off, as much as English has become an international language of digital expression, not surprisingly not everybody speaks it! That said, research and hands-on practice have shown that even Millenials and people who are generally comfortable with English, give a much better response when they receive advertising content in their own language.

What can I do for you?
  • Thorough keyword research performed in most European languages
  • Initial analysis of new market and estimation of impression volumes, average cost per click expected on Google search and budget suggestion.
  • My 7+ years of experience within online marketing have been dedicated mainly to the strategy and 360 degree management of international SEM campaigns.

Whether you're just starting out, or already have a PPC account running for some time, the possibilities for optimising campaign performance are nearly endless. I strongly believe in several principals definitive to success: there ought to be a good ongoing testing cycle in place; never make a decision based purely on intuition - there should be enough data to support it; always keep up with the latest and freshest in the industry.

Here are the core services I offer:
  • Planning, setup, execution and ongoing management of new campaigns/accounts.
  • Creation of ad creatives in most European languages. For the languages I don't speak, I work with native speakers who proofread my texts.
  • Free account audit and opportunities analysis. If you've been wondering for example whether you're making the best out of your budget or why you haven't received any leads lately, I can dig that out for you. It's all in there, you just need to know where to look!
